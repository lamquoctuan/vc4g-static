SORTING BY KARAT

The first thing we do is sort out items that are not gold or silver. To do this we use a Rare Earth Magnet. The Rare Earth Magnet is an incredibly powerful permanent magnet made from alloys of rare earth elements. Gold, Silver, and Platinum are not magnetic.

The second step in sorting involves checking for stamps. Many stamps are visible to the naked eye but some are stamped on the tiny posts of earrings or are too worn down to make out.
